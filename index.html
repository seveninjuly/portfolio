Little L

Laugh a little more, cheer up a little more, happy a little more! This app is a gif search tool with extra random jokes.

Users can search for different categories of gifs, if they don't find the one they love in the current results, they can always click the "want more?" button to get new ones. If they want to check out the jokes, they can click the "Little Extra" on the top to get random jokes. One joke will be displayed at a time, and just like the gifs, they can always use the "want more?" button to get another joke.
